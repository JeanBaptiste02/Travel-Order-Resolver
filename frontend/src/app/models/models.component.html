Yatra Models Overview

Yatra Core (current model)

Yatra Core is the essential model designed to process travel orders in text format as part of the Travel OrderResolver project.

Main Features:
  • Text analysis using NLP to extract crucial information: origin, destination, and the validity of the travel order.
  • Route optimization using graphs to find the best train connections.
  • Optional voice recognition to convert audio into text before processing.
  • Handling complex city names, even in the presence of typographical errors.
  • Optimized performance through fine-tuning techniques on a limited dataset.

This model focuses on understanding and accurately resolving travel orders while ensuring ease of implementation and robustness against text variations.

Yatra Boost

Yatra Boost is an advanced version leveraging a more robust dataset (currently under development).

Anticipated Features:
  • Improved contextual understanding through advanced NLP techniques and an enriched data set.
  • Enhanced error detection and management, even in complex grammatical structures.
  • More precise route optimization capabilities, considering factors like wait times.

This model will be ideal for applications requiring greater accuracy and depth of analysis.

Yatra Polyglot

Yatra Polyglot is designed to recognize and process travel orders in 20 different languages (currently under development).

Anticipated Features:
  • Support for multilingual recognition to cater to an international audience.
  • Multilingual NLP model capable of handling linguistic subtleties and cultural nuances.
  • Automatic language detection and adapted processing for better accuracy.

Ideal for global travel platforms looking to offer language accessibility.

Yatra Turbo

Yatra Turbo is the ultra-fast model, optimized for performance with complete data processing (currently under development).

Anticipated Features:
  • Extremely fast response times, suitable for real-time applications.
  • Optimized graph algorithms for instant route calculations.
  • Smooth handling of large data volumes while maintaining exceptional accuracy.

Ideal for services requiring high speed and efficiency, such as instant travel assistants.
